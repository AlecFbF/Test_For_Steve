Create your perfect Christmas bundle with Nintendo UK

To put Nintendo, and namely Amiibos! front of mind for Christmas, Nintendo UK approached us to see what we could do within Digital Display.

We created Digital Journey that started with the tease of the Amiibo collection, then if clicked through, they would be retargetted to be encourage create their perfect Nintendo bundle.

The neat thing was users could select items in banner that would then drop into the gift box below. Users could click out from the banner onto a Nintendo splash page in which users could see there bundle listed ready to purchase or send the "wishlist" to their loved ones for a little gift hint.

If the bundle was abandoned on site, the user would be retargetted with a reminder ad, to squeeze another possible conversion.
